# Octopus Energy

UK-headquartered renewable energy supplier and technology group. Famous for
its Kraken energy-management platform, which now licenses to other
utilities globally. The company has scaled rapidly from a single UK
supplier to a multi-country operation across Europe, North America, Asia,
and Australia.

Octopus is unusually engineering-led for a utility: the data platform sits
at the centre of pricing, demand forecasting, and customer operations.
Public commentary from their tech leadership emphasises AWS, Python, and
a strong opinion on data quality and observability.

The role I am applying to sits within the central data platform team and
supports both customer-facing analytics and Kraken's internal tooling.
