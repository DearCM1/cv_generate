# Senior Data Engineer

## About the role

We are looking for a Senior Data Engineer to join our growing data platform
team. You will own the design and operation of production data pipelines
that power our customer analytics, pricing, and operations products. You
will work closely with data scientists, ML engineers, and product managers
to deliver reliable, well-governed data to the rest of the business.

## What you'll do

- Design, build, and maintain production-grade ETL/ELT pipelines in Python,
  ingesting data from internal services, partners, and third-party APIs.
- Operate our cloud data infrastructure on AWS (Lambda, S3, ECS, Step
  Functions) with a strong emphasis on observability and cost discipline.
- Model data in our warehouse using SQL and dbt to support analytics,
  experimentation, and ML feature engineering.
- Establish and enforce data governance, lineage, and quality standards
  across the platform.
- Collaborate cross-functionally with stakeholders in operations,
  commercial, and product teams to translate business needs into reliable
  data products.
- Contribute to our LLM-driven automation work, including programmatic
  validation, structured content generation, and retrieval-augmented
  systems.

## What we're looking for

- Strong Python and SQL fundamentals; comfortable with pandas, NumPy, and
  modern data tooling.
- Hands-on experience building and operating production data pipelines on
  AWS (Lambda, S3 essential; Glue / EMR / Step Functions a plus).
- Working knowledge of dimensional modelling and warehouse design
  (Snowflake, BigQuery, or Redshift).
- Experience with data quality frameworks, governance, and lineage tools.
- A track record of working with non-technical stakeholders in an agile
  environment and shipping iteratively.
- Bonus: exposure to LLM-driven workflows, vector retrieval, and
  AI-assisted automation.

## What you'll get

- Competitive salary, equity, and pension.
- Remote-first culture with optional offices in London and Bristol.
- Real ownership of the data platform roadmap.
